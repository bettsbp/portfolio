About Me:

My name is Brandon and I'm very excited about all this coding and programming business. There are a couple things I'm nervous about, but hopefully that won't be much of a problem.

I was born in Vietnam and was almost immediately taken to an orphanage. I never knew my birth parents and I doubt I ever will, but that's okay because I love my family here and I'm glad they chose me. I've lived in a city called West Linn just a little ways away from Portland my entire life.

Before applying to this class I was pretty unsure of what I wanted to do with my life, you know, the usual "Oh no what am I going to do out of high school" kind of thing. It's a bit depressing to talk about, but I was in a bad place. ANYWAY, I took a couple coding classes in high school which all kind of glossed over the basics of html, css, javascript and a couple other things I don't remember anymore. Unfortunately, almost everything I learned in those classes I've purged from my memory. I wasn't too crazy about the idea of a four year degree college or a 'typical' career path. For the longest time, actually, my goal was to be a professional gamer! It's kind of weird, I know, but I did get pretty good. However, I'm now seeking a career where my chances of success are much greater and I can focus more on my skills rather than getting lucky.
